Brussels Responsible AI Networking Forum
Monday, April 28, 2025

About

The Brussels Responsible AI Networking Forum (BRAIN) is a one-day event that brings together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers from Brussels and beyond to discuss the latest advances in AI and its societal implications. The forum is organized with the support of the Brussels Institute of Advanced Studies (BrIAS), the AI for the Common Good Institute (FARI), and the Fund for Scientific Research (F.R.S.–FNRS).

  • Session 1 - Research

    This session will overview current research trends, covering aspects of transparency, fairness, explainability, safety, privacy, law, and generative AI/LLMs/foundational models. Expected contributions from invited researchers.

  • Session 2 - Practice

    This session presents the practical perspective. Expected presentations about best practices and open-source toolkits for responsible AI.

  • Session 3 - Policy

    This session will present national policies and EU's strategy for responsible AI.

  • Session 4 - Networking

    This session will facilitate networking among participants that can lead to future collaborations at the local, national, and EU level. It will be structured as parallel workshops with matchmaking among participants.
