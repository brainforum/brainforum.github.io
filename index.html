Brussels Responsible AI Networking Forum
Monday, April 28, 2025

About

The Brussels Responsible AI Networking Forum (BRAIN) is a one-day event that brings together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers from Belgium and beyond to discuss the latest advances in AI and its societal implications. The forum is organized with the support of the Brussels Institute of Advanced Studies (BrIAS), the AI for the Common Good Institute (FARI), and the Fund for Scientific Research (F.R.S.–FNRS).

Program

A detailed program is coming up.

  • Session 1 - Research

    This session provides an expert-driven overview of emerging research trends in responsible AI.

  • Session 2 - Practice

    This session brings together Belgium’s three AI research programs (FARI, FAIR, and TRAIL) to share their research insights and practical perspectives on responsible AI.

  • Session 3 - Policy

    This session includes presentations and a panel on the EU’s policy and strategic approach to responsible AI, featuring insights from the European AI Office.

  • Session 4 - Networking

    This session fosters networking among members of Belgium’s three AI research programmes and explores opportunities for collaboration.

Attend

Registration is free but required. The registration site will be available soon.
